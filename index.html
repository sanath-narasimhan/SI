THE CORE PHILOSOPHY

How the Model Works

Experiencing Realities

This course aims at experiencing the realities of the universe, bulk of which transcend the senses. By repeatedly experiencing them, one develops absolute faith in them and begins to perceive the universe as it is. Right perception leads to right decisions and then peace prevails.

Lecture & Practice

To arrive at these experiences of the realities of the universe, lecture, practice and mentoring sessions are conducted. Presentations on the teachings of the Upanishads form the lecture sessions. After each concept is presented, practice exercises are prescribed.

Mentoring Sessions

They are to be practiced through the week, the outcomes journaled and collated and reported during the weekly practice and mentoring session. During these sessions, each individual's report is discussed, tips are shared for bettering the practice of the concept.

Internalization

These mentoring sessions are repeated until the group reaches the targeted metric set for practice of the concept. Then a presentation of the next concept is made and practice sessions for that are repeated. This way, each concept of Vedanta is internalized.

This working model of gaining spiritual Intelligence is a doctoral thesis.
