About Us

Bridging ancient wisdom with modern living for a more purposeful life.

OUR FOUNDATION

The SRG School of Vedanta

The SRG School of Vedanta, founded by Dr. Usha Narasimhan, is dedicated to making the timeless wisdom of Vedanta practical and relevant for modern living. Our programs help individuals understand themselves better, manage stress, and lead peaceful, meaningful, and fulfilling lives.

Rooted in authentic Vedantic teaching, we bridge ancient knowledge with contemporary challenges such as anxiety, fear, and emotional imbalance. Through structured, application-oriented learning, participants develop clarity, resilience, and inner stability.

With over a hundred individuals trained, SRG School of Vedanta continues to nurture a growing community committed to self-discovery, inner growth, and lasting well-being.

👁️
VISION

Empowering Growth

To empower every individual — students, professionals, homemakers, and spiritual seekers — to unlock their inner strength and live with clarity, resilience, and sustained joy.

🚀
MISSION

Practical Wisdom

To make Spiritual Intelligence practical and accessible through a structured 5-stage journey that combines ancient wisdom with modern tools, daily reflection, and compassionate mentorship.

OUR FACULTY

Meet Our Mentors

FOUNDER & PRINCIPAL FACULTY

Dr. Usha Narasimhan

Ph.D. (Vedanta), M.A., M.Phil., Vidvat, B.Tech (IIT Chennai)

Dr. Usha Narasimhan is a dedicated spiritual aspirant, educator, and thought leader in the field of Vedanta, committed to guiding individuals toward a peaceful, meaningful, and fulfilling life. She is the founder of the SRG School of Vedanta, an initiative that has successfully trained over a hundred individuals in the practical application of Vedantic wisdom for inner balance and clarity.

With a strong academic foundation, Dr. Usha Narasimhan holds a B.Tech degree from IIT Chennai and has pursued advanced studies in Sanskrit and Vedanta, earning an M.A., M.Phil., Vidvat, and a Doctorate from Karnataka Sanskrit University. Her unique combination of technical education and deep philosophical training enables her to effectively bridge ancient wisdom with modern-day challenges.

Prior to her full-time engagement in spiritual teaching, she worked as a project management consultant and PMP trainer. Her courses emphasize practical application and inner transformation, helping participants manage stress and build emotional resilience.

Smt. Sowmya Rajagopal

Learning Designer & Mentor

Smt. Sowmya Rajagopal is a learning designer with over 20 years of experience, including 8+ years in eLearning and prior experience as an embedded systems designer. A PMP-certified professional with a background in Electronics and Communication Engineering, she brings a structured yet creative approach to learning. Currently pursuing a Bachelor’s in Viśiṣṭādvaita Vedānta, she contributes as a mentor to the Spiritual Intelligence Program.

Smt. Sujatha Varadharajan

Mentor

With over 18 years of global corporate experience across leading MNCs, Sujatha brings a unique blend of operational excellence and spiritual depth to the role of Mentor. Known for her people-centric leadership, she combines corporate insight with a grounded understanding of human values. Over the past few years, she has been deeply studying and practicing Vedanta under the guidance of Dr. Usha Narasimhan.
