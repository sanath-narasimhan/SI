THE ORIGIN

Grace of the Preceptors

Sri Rangagopal school of Vedanta has been set up by the grace of the preceptors of **Dr. Usha Narasimhan**. After having studied Vedanta and passing the certification examinations in Vishishtadvaita, she was urged by her preceptor **Dr. N. S. Anantharangachar**, Vedanta Vidvan, to spread this authentic, traditional, philosophical knowledge.

The mission of the school is to ensure that the "desirous deserving" can benefit from the deep wisdom of our ancestors, blending heritage with modern relevance.

Dr. Usha Narasimhan

Founder & Principal Faculty

IIT Madras Alumna (B.Tech 1986), PhD in Spiritual Intelligence from Karnataka Sanskrit University.
